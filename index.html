HISTORY

The Port Harcourt Electricity Distribution Company (PHED) was established in 2005 following the unbundling of the Power Holding Company of Nigeria (PHCN). It became a privately held company in 2013. PHED is responsible for distributing electricity in Akwa Ibom, Bayelsa, Cross River, and Rivers states.
